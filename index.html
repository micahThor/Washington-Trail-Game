=== Welcome to the Trail ===

Imagine a world where every morning you get that sudden realization that you overslept your alarm. Before you check the time you know you are going to be late. Well the developers at Washington Trail Games Company are proud to capture these panicked moments into an interactive simulation. See if you got what it takes to survive the commute.

=== Here are the rules ===

It's a long way to your destination in Seattle. At the beginning of the game you will be alotted a set amount of money, time, and health to make this perilous journey. There are five stages to navigate as you endure the harsh and unforgiving nature of local traffic. You will be forced to make difficult choices on this journey, so beware. If you can muster your wit, you just might make it to Seattle in time.
